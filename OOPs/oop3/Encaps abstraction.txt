Encapsulation :-
It is the process of wrapping up the implementations of the data members and methods in a class

Abstraction :- 
It hides unnessary details and show the necessary details.
It can be achieved by abstract classes and interfaces.

Encapsulation vs Abstraction :-
Abstraction focuses on external things and encapsulation works on internal things
Abstraction is the process of gaining information, encapsulaton is the  process of containing the information.

Data hiding vs Encapsulation :-
Data hiding focuses on data security , encapsulation focuses on hiding unnecessary information.
In data hiding the data we hide should be private andshould not be accessed directly, in encapsulation the data can beanything public protected etc.
